Career & Industrial Exposure

Industrial Energy Audits

Our members gain elite field experience by conducting professional audits. To date, we have completed 20+ audits at high-stakes locations including the Reserve Bank of India (Fort) and Godrej BKC.

Placement Benefit: Practical knowledge of audit tools and reporting is a top-tier skill for Industrial Engineer roles.

Placement Preparation

Through our Energy Audits and Managers seminars, we prepare students for Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) certifications. This technical edge has directly impacted member readiness for core engineering placements.

Future-Ready Skills

We focus on the future of energy. Our workshops on Advanced Hydrogen Technology and On-Grid Solar Systems ensure that our members are not just ready for today’s jobs, but for the green-tech revolution of 2030.
